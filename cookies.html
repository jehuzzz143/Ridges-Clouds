WHAT ARE THE COOKIES ×

cookies are pieces of information saved about you when you’re online, and they track you as you browse. So say you go to a weather website and put in your zip code to look up what’s happening in your area; the next time you visit the same site, it will remember your zip code because of cookies.


If the website cookies are rejected, the booking function in website will not work. but you can just keep on browsing but will unable to login.


By accepting cookies users will never be harmed, it will just help the website algorithm to not input data again when the website already detected the data.
